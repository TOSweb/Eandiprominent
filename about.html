    ABOUT US

    With our knowledge
    we guarantee success

    Welcome to E And I Prominent India Pvt. Ltd., where craftsmanship meets innovation to bring your visions to life. We are more than just a construction company; we are the architects of your dreams. Formed by the merger of E-Prominent Consultants (since 2019), SH Engineering Consultant (since 2017), and Bright Enterprises (since 1994), our legacy spans decades.

    Our unwavering commitment to excellence ensures we deliver projects of the highest quality, always on time and within budget. Your satisfaction is our driving force, and we pride ourselves on building long-term relationships based on trust and reliability. Experience the exceptional with E And I Prominent India Pvt. Ltd. — where every project is a testament to our passion for perfection and dedication to your success.

    Mission

    Our mission is to exceed expectations through innovation, research, and unparalleled service. We set industry benchmarks and aim to become India's leading design and infrastructure construction company, known for creativity and continuous innovation. Driven by perfection and a passion for pushing boundaries, we transform ideas into reality. At E And I Prominent India Pvt. Ltd., we don't just build structures; we build legacies

    Vision

    At E And I Prominent India Pvt. Ltd., we build lasting relationships with our customers through exceptional service and cutting-edge innovation. Rooted in honesty and integrity, our ethical practices permeate every facet of our work. Trust us to exceed your expectations as we set new benchmarks in customer satisfaction.
